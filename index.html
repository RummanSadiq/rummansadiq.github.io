This is Programmers/Tech Community

A community focused on tech industry news and latest development in software technologies as well as hardware and general science developments.

Community Guidelines

no prolonged discussions, no non-related posts

A lot of professional engineers are part of this group only because they know that we have very strict spam policy which does not tolerate any non-related stuff, because their every minute is precious so, no cat fights or non-related posts.

1 Warning Rule

You will get only 1 warning and next time you will be removed from the group if you post any non-related stuff and/or you start any debate/prolonged discussions here. You can always chat with people in private about something but not in the group.

Be Responsible

Please be very responsible in what you share here. We want maximum people to take benefit from each other. So don't make admins take extreme measure to maintain decorum.

We have got a few more admins in the group. You can check the group details to see who they are. Admins responsibility is to keep an eye for anything non-related and remove anyone who is not willing to follow the community guidelines.


Happy learning!
